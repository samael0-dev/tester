Muichiro Tokito
Breathing style: MIST BREATHING
Muichiro Tokito is a major supporting character of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. He is a Demon Slayer of the Demon Slayer Corps and the current Mist Hashira. Muichiro along with his brother Yuichiro Tokito, are also the descendants of Upper Rank One, Kokushibo.
